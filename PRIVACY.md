# Privacy Policy

OpenCC is designed with privacy by default.

## What We Collect

OpenCC does not collect any personal data or usage data.

## Internet Connection

OpenCC does not make internet connections for data collection, tracking, analytics, or telemetry.

## Data Stored

OpenCC only stores necessary app settings required for functionality.

## Where Data Is Stored

All stored settings remain on your local device.

## Data Sharing

No data is sent to external servers, and no stored data leaves your device.
